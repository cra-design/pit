Donations and gifts on a personal tax return

  • Who can claim
  • Gifts you can claim
  • How much you can claim
  • How to claim

Who can claim

  • Tax year: 2024| Prior tax years: 2022 and earlier
  • Federal tax return: Line 34900
  • Provincial or territorial tax return (if applicable): Line 58969

You or your spouse or common-law partner may be able to claim a tax credit on your tax return for a gift or other property made to a registered charity or qualified donee.

Eligibility

To claim a donation or gift on a personal tax return, all of the following must apply:

  • All of the above is needed to be eligible

    You or another person must meet all of the criteria to claim child expenses.

  • All of the above is needed to be eligible

    You or another person must meet all of the criteria to claim child expenses.

  • Based on your selections above:

    Child care expenses may be claimed

    You or another person may claim child care expenses as a deduction for an eligible child.
